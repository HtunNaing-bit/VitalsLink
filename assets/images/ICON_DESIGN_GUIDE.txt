VITALS_LINK APP ICON - QUICK REFERENCE GUIDE
============================================

DESIGN ELEMENTS:
----------------
1. White rounded square background
2. Dark gray/black circle (center) with radial gradient
3. Faint grid/radar pattern overlay
4. White tick marks around circle perimeter
5. White waveform (V shape) - health metrics
6. Lime green upward trend/checkmark - positive progress
7. Lime green dot at bottom center

COLORS:
-------
- White: #FFFFFF
- Dark Gray: #1A1A1A to #2A2A2A (gradient)
- Lime Green: #00FF00 to #7FFF00 (gradient)

SIZES NEEDED:
-------------
- 1024x1024 (master file)
- All iOS sizes (auto-generated)
- All Android sizes (auto-generated)

FILE LOCATION:
--------------
- assets/images/app_icon.png (1024x1024)
- assets/images/app_icon.svg (vector version)

AFTER CREATING ICON:
--------------------
1. Place app_icon.png in assets/images/
2. Run: flutter pub run flutter_launcher_icons
3. Test on devices

See VITALS_LINK_ICON_SPECIFICATION.md for full details.
